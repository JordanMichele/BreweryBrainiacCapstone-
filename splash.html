Welcome to The Brewery Brainiac Blog

This site is a simple craft beer blog, calling extra attention to local content and information in the Rochester New York Area!!


This app is intended to be a one-stop-shop for craft beer lovers and enthusiast in the Rochester NY area. Giving users information on new beer alerts, events, new breweries, ect. Once inside, you'll gain access to the interactive map with all the local craft breweries in Rochester NY. Stay tuned because The Brewery Brainac plans to expand this app to Buffalo, Syracuse, NYC and eventually USA as a whole, CHEERS!!
